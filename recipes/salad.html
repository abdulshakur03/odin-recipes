Salad Recipe

Salad

Description

This is a recipe for a vegetable salad

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Cucumber
  • Red beans
  • Green Peas

Steps

  1. Cut the cabbage
  2. Shred the Carrots
  3. Dice the Cucumber
  4. Mix all the ingredients together
  5. Pure in the red beans and green peas and mix well
  6. Add mayonnaise if desired
  7. Enjoy!
